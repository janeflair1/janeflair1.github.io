Corn Bread & Caviar

Cornbread and Caviar is a full service caterer specializing in all types of events from the casual to the complex with distinctive menus featuring fresh and natural goods from local suppliers. Our dedicated and experienced staff has been creating perfect moments since 1981.

SERVICES

Corprate Events

Our corporate team is experienced at handling everything from small meetings to large seminars, company picnics, holiday parties, and elegant special events. Our goal is to create the perfect event from initial menu consultation to drop off delivery or set-up, serving and clean up.

Wedding Events

Our wedding team works to customize menus, presentation and service to ensure that your event reflects your style and vision while remaining within the parameters of your budget. From the initial menu consultation and tasting to the event itself, our mission is to create a worry free, but memorable day.
